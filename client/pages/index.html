How it works

Parked in 4 simple steps

ParkIQ removes every friction point from the parking experience.

01

Search your destination

Enter your address or use GPS. See all available spots on the live map within your chosen radius.

02

Pick & book your spot

Choose by price, rating, or features. Lock your slot and pay securely — card, UPI, or wallet.

03

Arrive & scan QR

Drive to your reserved spot. Scan your unique QR code at the gate — no ticket, no delay.

04

Leave & auto-pay

Scan out when done. Any difference from your estimate is automatically settled or refunded.
